NFTropolis

NFTropolis is an immersive virtual world built on three.js technology. Each NFT minted triggers a unique dialogue exchange with one of the Non-Player Characters (NPCs), featuring emotion-rich speech synthesis. These NPC dialogues are designed to introduce a new layer of narrative and choice-driven decision-making, making each user's experience truly one-of-a-kind. By participating in this cutting-edge virtual world, users can immerse themselves in a dynamic and ever-evolving storyline that responds directly to their NFT creations, creating a captivating and engaging experience unlike any other.

 

Virtual Reality Illusion Generator

The Virtual Reality Illusion Generator is a web-based application that uses the latest web audio and three.js libraries to replicate Diana Deutsch's Phantom Words illusions in a virtual environment. With this tool, users can explore and experience the intriguing phenomenon of hearing words or sounds that are not actually present in the audio stimulus. The VRIG offers a unique and interactive way to engage with auditory illusions and provides a valuable educational and research tool for students, researchers, and anyone interested in cognitive neuroscience and auditory perception.
